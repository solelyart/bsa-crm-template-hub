üìã Getting Started with Power Automate

1

Access Power Automate

Go to flow.microsoft.com and sign in with your Microsoft 365 work or school account.

2

Create Your First Flow

Click "Create" and choose "Automated cloud flow" for event-triggered automation or "Scheduled cloud flow" for time-based automation.

3

Add a Trigger

Select what starts your flow: an email arriving, a meeting ending, a scheduled time, or a file being created.

4

Add Actions

Add the OneNote connector and choose "Create page in a section" or "Update page content". Connect your notebook and format your content.

5

Test and Enable

Use the "Test" button to verify your flow works correctly, then save and enable it for automatic execution.
